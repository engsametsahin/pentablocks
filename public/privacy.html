PentaBlocks Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-04-08

What We Store

PentaBlocks stores gameplay data in your browser using local storage. This can include completed levels, best times, and local player stats.

Analytics

PentaBlocks may collect basic analytics events such as session start, level start, level completion, and game flow interactions to improve gameplay and product quality.

Advertising

If ads are enabled in future releases, ad display pacing is limited and intended to avoid disrupting onboarding. Additional ad controls may depend on your consent selection.

Data Retention

Local gameplay data remains in your browser unless you clear browser storage. Future account-based features may introduce cloud storage for scores and progression.

Contact

For policy questions, contact the project owner through the official distribution channel where PentaBlocks is published.
