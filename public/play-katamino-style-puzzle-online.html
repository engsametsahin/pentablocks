Play Katamino-Style Puzzle Online

PentaBlocks is a free browser puzzle game inspired by Katamino-style piece-fitting gameplay. Place geometric blocks, beat the timer, and improve your best score on each level.

You can play solo through level progression or race a friend in multiplayer with a challenge code. The puzzle seed is shared in multiplayer, so both players solve the exact same board.
